### 3. Currency-constraint duality across closure layers

#### 3.1 The currency-constraint principle
State the central morphism: lower-layer currency induces higher-layer feasibility constraints. Present this as the forward half of the duality law across closure layers. Tie the statement to package morphisms rather than informal analogy.

#### 3.2 Shadow prices as emergent higher-layer currencies
State the reverse half: once higher-layer constraints bind, shadow prices emerge as higher-layer currencies. Connect this directly to the lambda recovery experiments. Emphasize that emergence here is operational and measurable.

#### 3.3 Canonical examples
Give the conceptual exemplars: energy to temperature/free energy, bits to rate-distortion multipliers, budget to price, and compute to regularization pressure. Use these as orientation anchors, not as full empirical case studies. Reinforce the shared dual structure across domains.

#### 3.4 Why this is a closure law rather than a metaphor
Argue that the principle is structural because it tracks feasibility and maintenance of packaged objects under bounded spending. Distinguish this from superficial vocabulary transfer between fields. Ground the claim in measurable closure behavior.

### 5. Results

#### 5.1 Honest audits under coarse-graining
Present the DPI-style empirical result that coarse observation does not fabricate directionality currencies. Emphasize seed-robust nonviolation within tolerance. Interpret this as an audit honesty condition.

#### 5.2 Price emergence and slack
Present the budget sweep showing near-perfect anti-monotonic relation between budget and lambda. Highlight explicit slack-tail behavior where lambda collapses to zero. Interpret this as emergent price only when constraints bind.

#### 5.3 Currency dimension grows with resolution
Present ladder results showing increasing cycle-rank capacity from coarse to mid to fine resolution. Explain this as growth in independent currency coordinates as lens resolution increases. Note the distinction between cycle-space appearance and active affinity magnitude.

#### 5.4 Proxy currencies fail
Show that structurally aligned costs outperform arbitrary proxy costs on held-out prediction. Report that proxy lambdas are less stable across seeds. Use this as the core falsification-style ablation.

#### 5.5 Budgets buy object stability
Show that stronger budget enforcement reduces packaging defect across the sweep. Report monotone defect decline with increasing lambda. Interpret this as spending purchasing closure coherence.
